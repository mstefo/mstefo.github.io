Thermal Rift

What Is Thermal Rift?

Thermal Rift is a 2D puzzle platformer that involves the player manipulating the world's temperature to solve challenges and progress. The player can switch between the 3 states (Normal, Hot and Cold) which will change the effect of the player's weapon and the state of the certain objects. This project was designed with the intention of creating a vertical slice within a short time period for university.

Key Contributions

In general, my contributions to Thermal Rift were refining the core gameplay concepts, developing some puzzle objects and their interactions with the player and environment and designing the levels and puzzles that the player must overcome. I achieved this through developing mock ups for levels and gameplay concepts and assisting the lead programmer with certain scripts.
