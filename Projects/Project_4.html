Recomposition

What Is Recomposition?

Recomposition is a 2D puzzle game where the player must draw connections with the pairing "life forces". This game was developed as a submission for the MQ Revive Game Jam. It follows the theme "Revive" by having the player make the connections for specific plants in order to reconnect their life force.

Key Contributions

My contributions to Recomposition involved creating the high concept, setting up the grid system, the drawing of lines controls and the end result screen. In addition, I worked closely with other team members to ensure the project was aligned with the Game Jam restrictions and remained in scope.
