About Me

I am a dedicated individual with a passion in programming. With a knack in challenging myself, I always look forward to learning new things to improve my existing skills. This is reflected in how I spend some of my free time taking on personal projects and adapting the newly learned concepts to them.


I have a strong passion for creating new experiences, whether it be for entertainment purposes such as video games or by establishing an innovative application to satisfy a particular need. In any project, I consider all the possible approaches to meet the requirements with a strong focus on the end-user, asking myself key questions such as how they would like the software to work or how it should interact with them.
